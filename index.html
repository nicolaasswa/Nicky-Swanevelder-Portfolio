About me

My fascination with tech started way back in 2004 as I was completing a graphic design certification that touched on Flash based websites. Flash was the bomb back then with animations, sounds and cool effects. At that stage though my passion was in print so web was put on hold.

Fast forward to 2011, I am still in print but my love for tech and the outdoors inspires me to create my first Wordpress website. From here, the Wordpress bug bites and I created several websites using the WP platform - Aurora HR, The Spine Clinic and JF Law. During this time period, wanting to do more customised WP websites and features, I self study HTML and CSS using Youtube, Freecodecamp.org and whatever content I could find.

Fast forward to 2018, and the need to include more functionality to my skill set, I started the Full Stack Web Developer Bootcamp by Hyperion Dev. I will complete this Bootcamp at the end of 2018. On completion my skill set will include full stack development including Javascript, Python, In-depth PHP, Django Framework and App Development.

After completing the Full Stack Web Developer Bootcamp, I will continue my studies working towards completing the Mobile Developer Bootcamp with specialisation in Android development.

In my personal life, I am the husband to my beautiful wife and father to an amazing boy. In my free time and over weekends, we spend our time outdoors, run (when we have time) and love to explore our country.
